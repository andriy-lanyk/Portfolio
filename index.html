My Story

It all started in the automn of 2020. At that time, I didn’t know quite much about programming. Then I became increasingly concerned about my future, like what will I be doing since 2, 3 or 5 years? Everything started to make sense to me after I built some dummy colorful html pages(with simple css styles). I knew why I wrote those code and I knew exactly what every single line of code does. That felt so right. They seem dummy to me today, but at that time I was really proud of myself. Now I do not want to stop and learn new technologies so that I can write modern and attractive code.
